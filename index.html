About

Hello. I'm a technologist and legal researcher. My work at the internet Research Lab focuses on information and communication technology, law, and human rights.

I also co-chair the Public Interest Technology Group and serve on the Advisory Board of the critical infrastructure lab.

In the past, I've worked as the interim head of ARTICLE 19's Global Digital Programme; served as a member of the Progressive International secretariat; documented methods of censorship-resistance as an Iran Cyber Dialogue Fellow; studied online censorship in Asia as an Information Controls Fellow; led research on internet governance and civil liberties at the Centre for Internet and Society; and worked as a Legislative Assistant to Member of Parliament Fellow.
