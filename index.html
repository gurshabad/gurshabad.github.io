About

Hello. I am a technologist and legal researcher based in Bangalore, India. My research focuses on network security, privacy and political repression.

I currently work as Development Coordinator in the Progressive International secretariat, and serve as co-chair of the Public Interest Technology Group.

In the past, I've studied online censorship in Asia as a Information Controls Fellow; written on internet governance and civil liberties at the Centre for Internet and Society; and worked as a Legislative Assistant to Member of Parliament (LAMP) Fellow.
